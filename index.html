Key Elements of a BTS Program

Duration

Higher education studies with a professional focus, generally lasting 4 semesters

Internships

Studies combining theoretical instruction and practical internships in companies

ECTS

Studies organized into modules and validated with ECTS credits

Professional Sector

A study program jointly developed by the high school and the relevant professional sectors and accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education and Research (MESR)

Diploma

A national diploma

Industry Partners

Internships, apprenticeships, and job placement

Advantages of a BTS Program

Bac+2 (Associate Degree Level) with the Possibility to Continue Studies

A BTS allows students to obtain a Bac+2 level diploma, opening the door to further higher education

Classes of 12 to 16 Students Maximum

BTS classes are generally limited to 12 to 16 students, allowing personalized support

Stronger Contact with Teachers

The small class size encourages more direct interaction and better guidance from instructors

More Practical Opportunities

Students gain hands-on experience through internships, projects, and real professional situations

Strong Sense of Community

Students benefit from a strong group spirit and collaboration within their BTS program
